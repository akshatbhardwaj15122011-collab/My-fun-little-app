Battery Survivor

A fast-paced arcade survival game set inside a dying smartphone.

You play as a tiny living battery fighting against chaos inside an overloaded operating system. Dodge aggressive apps, survive endless waves of digital threats, and collect charge particles to keep the phone alive for just a little longer. Humanity somehow turned “having 73 background apps open” into a survival genre. Inspiring stuff.
